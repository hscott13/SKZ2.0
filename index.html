Who are Stray Kids?

Stray Kids is a South Korean Boy Band consisting of 8 members formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017. They have released 10 albums, 11 EPs and 28 singles. Their music is generally K-pop, hip hop and electrtonic based. They are considered a 'self producing' idol group as the members themselves are almost always involved in the writing, composing and arranging process. Similar to other K-pop groups, Stray Kids have a strong fan following and fans refer to themselves as 'Stays'.
